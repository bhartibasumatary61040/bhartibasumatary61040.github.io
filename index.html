About Me

I’m Bharati Basumatary, a passionate and detail-oriented QA Engineer with over 5+ years of experience in Manual and Automation Testing. I specialize in ensuring high-quality software by identifying defects early and improving overall application performance and reliability.

I am from Assam and hold a B.Tech in Computer Science from Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow. I am also an ISTQB Certified Tester, which reflects my strong understanding of software testing principles, best practices, and industry standards.

I have hands-on experience in functional testing, regression testing, and API validation. I specialize in automation using Selenium WebDriver with Java and TestNG, and I have developed scalable automation frameworks using the Page Object Model (POM) design pattern.

Additionally, I have experience in API testing using Postman and Rest Assured, where I validate responses, status codes, and HTTP methods to ensure API reliability.

I have worked in Agile environments, collaborating closely with developers, product owners, and cross-functional teams to deliver high-quality products within deadlines.
